Design is a matter of content presentation under constraints. A designer have to use its knowledge to compose a site presentation that respect expectations of both, the client and the user.

Because screen reading can be exhausting, it's recommended to style text with a comfortable line-height and a line length between 45 and 75 characters. While writing, it's better to express ideas through simple sentences in short paragraphs.
